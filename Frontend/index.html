Climate Change

Climate change harms agriculture: rising temperatures affect crops, altered precipitation leads to water scarcity, and shifts impact pests, diseases, and livestock health.

Crop Management

Crop management optimizes crop growth, covering soil prep, planting, irrigation, nutrient management, pest control, and harvesting. Timely irrigation and fertilization tailored to soil and crop needs are essential.

Food Insecurity

Climate changes like droughts, floods, and extreme weather harm crops, leading to food shortages, price volatility, and vulnerability. Poor infrastructure and resource competition worsen food insecurity.

Pesticides

Pesticide use has small-scale impacts on the land, affecting soil health and potentially contributing to environmental issues.
